Enigma

Так казав Заратустра

Author: F. Nietzsche

Genre: philosophical novel

Year of writing: 1896

is a philosophical three-volume novel-treatise by Friedrich Nietzsche, written in 1883-1885, which revolves around the ideas of eternal repetition, the "death of God" and the prophecy of the superman. The main character is the Zoroastrian prophet Zarathustra, reinterpreted by Nietzsche, who returns to humanity with a sermon after a long seclusion in the mountains.
